About Us


Maka is a local-express delivery company on two wheels, which provides delivery services throughout the city. In today's world everyone is busy with infinite number of works and no one has time to complete all their tasks. Here we comes. Maka provides you with easy and reliable delivery services with guaranteed product delivery from one place to another within the city. We are just fingertip away to your work. Book a Slot in maka, our delivery person will collect and delivers your item within 2 hours.
Our main objective and motto is to move fast and provide best and reliable services.
Aim of Maka is to offer best pricing and services to our clients and customers. We are here to make sure that your package is in safe hands.
